WORKSHOP ON APP DEVELOPMENT

ACM student Chapter, Chitkara University in collaboration with Google Education organized its six days workshop on ‘App Development’. 22 students participated in the workshop.

The six-days workshop unpacked what constitutes a great learning concepts of CS to gain a hands on experience in the field of App development . Workshop contents:

Applied CS skill course with the help of projects like ‘Scarne’s Dice’ and ‘Binary search tree and AVL’ .

Major takeaways:

An understanding of desirable practices in App development. Looking forward to code with greater confidence.

Under the guidance of Mr Saravjeet the workshop was organised by the students of acm society Shalvi Saxena and Hitesh Bansal.
